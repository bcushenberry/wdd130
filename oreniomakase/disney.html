Disney in Japan

Disney has long been hugely popular in Japan, which was the second country in the world to get its Disney theme park and the first outside of the US.
As such, there is a large variety of merchandise to be found, including exclusive lineups available only at Disney Sea - Japan's unique Disney park located next to Tokyo Disneyland.
Additionally, there are Disney stores in and around the greater Tokyo area, as well as the Disney Flagship Store in downtown Tokyo, which offers many items year-round and its own exclusive lineups.

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to introduce Ore Ni Omakase, a personal shopping / export business, to potential customers. It will include a main page that describes the nature of the business and how it works; Disney and Starbucks pages that show examples of product lines offered by the business and a description of each product line; and a Contact Us page that lists ways to get in touch with the business owner.

Audience

The audience is anyone who wants to buy things from Japan but who does not currently live in Japan, or anyone who lives in Japan but does not have access to the products offered.

Content

Ore ni Omakase

Whether you've been to Japan before and are feeling nostalgic for some senbei, or if you've never been but would like to dip your feet in the water, we've got you covered! At Ore ni Omakase, we offer personal shopping services for everything from popular best-sellers and Japan-exclusive merchandise to the latest snacks and trinkets available in the Land of the Rising Sun.

How Does It Work?
How does it work, you ask? It's simple! Just take a look at our offerings on Instagram and leave a comment or shoot us a message on anything that looks appealing. Don't see the thing that you want? Not a problem! You can let us know about whatever you want to order, and we'll track it down for you! Enjoy a taste of Japan delivered straight to your front door!

Where Do I Get Started?
Feel free to browse through more of the site, and when you're ready to place an order or make an inquiry, you can click on the "Contact Us" button below, which will take you straight to our Instagram. We look forward to hearing from you!

Disney Exclusives

Disney in Japan
Disney is hugely popular in Japan, which is made abundantly apparent from the many exclusives that are launched here. While sm-home are released in stores such as the Disney Flagship Store in downtown Tokyo, the majority of the exclusives are reserved for the Disney parks themselves.

We've Got You Covered!
But no need to worry! Ore ni omakase! ("Leave it to me!") We take regular trips to Disneyland and Disney Sea and make sure we're there for every major launch during the year. What's more, we have immediate access to the Flagship Store and visit it regularly. If you're hankering for the latest Mickey merch or want to spend a bit more time with Duffy and Friends, we've got you covered!

Starbucks Exclusives

Starbucks in Japan
Starbucks tumblers have long been the object of many a collector's eye, and the coffee giant's seasonal offerings in Japan could comfortably fit in anyone's collection! From exclusive designs that incorporate the creative, adorable aesthetic found throughout Japanese pop culture, to special exclusive crossovers like the recent Snoopy lineup, there's sure to be something for everyone, coffee lover or not!

The Starbucks Reserve Roastery
In addition to the regular Starbucks merchandise, there is also a special Starbucks Roastery in Tokyo that features exclusive merchandise of its own! Positioned as a higher-end type of Starbucks, the tumblers and other products offered exhibit a certain flair that differentiate them from standard Starbucks fare.
